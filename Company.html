Sets of NFC and RFID microchips

Each item comes with a set of microchips, which allows to write or copy information. You don't need to know the frequency of your ID card or door keys, because the microchips we provide are widely used on the market .Smart ring includes set of 2 microchips (1 NFC and 1 RFID)

Smart bracelet is going with set of 2 microchips ( 1 NFC and 1 RFID)  or set of 4 microchips (2 NFC and 2 RFID)  or set of 6 microchips  (3 NFC and 3 RFID) 

Smart Key holder is going with a set of 8 microchips  (4 NFC and 4 RFID)

*  RFID and NFC microchips are rewriteable.
